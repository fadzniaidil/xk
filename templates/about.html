About

EduSphere helps Sijil Pelajaran Malaysia (SPM) graduates in choosing the program for their degree level. The programs are limited to engineering (mechanical, electric and electronic, civil, chemical) and Information Technology (IT) program. The aim of this web application is to suggest program in the university which is suitable for the SPM graduates based on their SPM result.
